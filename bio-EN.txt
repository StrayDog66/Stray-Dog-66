═══════════════════════════════════════════════
LONG BIO (250 words) — FOR PRESS KIT, INTERVIEWS, BOOKING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

Stray Dog —Perro Callejero— is the alias of a producer
and DJ born in Pilar, Paraguay, and based in Panama
City. His path reflects two geographies and two sensibilities:
the slow border of the Paraguay River and the electric
pulse of the Panamanian tropics. It's that contrast — the
southern nostalgia with the Caribbean humidity — that
shapes a sound rejecting polish in favor of rawness, and
samples in favor of real machines.

In 2026 he founds 66 RECORDS, a label dedicated to hard
techno and industrial from Latin America. His debut EP
"Feral Frequency" (SXD-066) is in preparation: hard
techno and industrial recorded between Pilar and Panama,
without sample-packs or quantized drums. Limited edition
in planning.

His schedule includes two recurring radio shows:
THE GROWLER, a bi-weekly Thursday session where he shares
deep music with a beer in hand, and OFF LEASH, the weekly
Friday warm-up for those heading out to the dancefloor.
Both shows are published on SoundCloud and Mixcloud with
full tracklists.

His operating range runs 125-150 BPM. On the dial:
hard techno, industrial, dub techno, dark bass, and
late-night house. Each week he publishes "On Rotation",
a curated selection of tracks from other artists hitting
hard in his playlist. His philosophy: no leash, no master,
no mercy — but with good musical taste.
